SWAC

SmartWebApplicationComponents

This template is the startpoint for your great webpage!

Easy to use

Smart stands for: Easy to use!

SWAC is just as easy to use, as HTML. You only have to add an script to your site and than place one special HTML Tag, wherever you want to use a SWAC component.

Chooseable

Smart stands for: You have the choice!

SWAC gives you ready to use components. But if you want to, you can customise it. Customisation goes in levels from easy configuration to extension programming.

Datadriven

Smart stands for: Concentrate on your data

SWAC components are datadriven. You can concentrate your work on your data. For most goals isn't even a configuration needed. The components get the best out of your data.

Scaleable

Smart stands for: Scaleable

Only the components you use on your page are transfered to the client, to save your bandwith and make your application quickly available to your customers.

SWACs components can work with single datasets or with a mass of datasets.

Designable

Smart stands for: Designable

SWAC is based on the UIkit designframework. You can choose from the UIkit designs or even create your own design. Furthermore all components can be redesigned without the knowledge of the underlying code.

Developer friendly

Smart stands for: Developer friendly

SWAC has an debugmode that shows you where you possible use a component the wrong way or when your data does not fit the components expectations.

SWAC has also an hint mode that give you hints on how to customise components.
